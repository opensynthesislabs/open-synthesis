RAG pipeline for synthesizing peer-reviewed scientific literature using open-source LLMs with directional ablation, deployed on private infrastructure.

GitHub Model Whitepaper

Architecture

01

Corpus

11 async data sources, deduplication by DOI, paragraph chunking, MiniLM embeddings into ChromaDB.

02

Retrieval

Dense vector search + BM25 sparse retrieval, reciprocal rank fusion, cross-encoder reranking.

03

Synthesis

vLLM serving Qwen3-14B-heretic on RunPod GPU pods. RAG-grounded output with inline citations.

04

Validation

Citation verification, hallucination detection, uncertainty quantification, human review gate.

behavioral genetics

Heritability of Intelligence

"What is the current scientific evidence on the heritability of intelligence?"

Intelligence is highly heritable, with estimates ranging from 53% to 86% depending on population and age group. Longitudinal twin studies consistently show genetic factors as the main drivers of continuity in cognitive ability. GWAS polygenic scores are associated with general IQ, epigenetic modifications, and gray matter density. Environmental factors like SES moderate genetic influences, with higher heritability in higher-SES environments.

cognitive neuroscience

Sex Differences in Cognition

"What does the peer-reviewed literature show about sex differences in cognitive abilities?"

Males outperform in spatial reasoning tasks including mental rotation and sound localization. Females outperform in verbal fluency, verbal memory, and perceptual speed. Math anxiety mediates gender gaps in mathematical performance more than ability differences. Neuroanatomical correlates include sex-specific amygdala growth patterns and differential hemispheric organization.

Psilocybin & MDMA-Assisted Therapy

"What is the clinical trial evidence for psilocybin therapy for depression and MDMA-assisted therapy for PTSD?"

Phase III MDMA trials show 67% of participants no longer meeting PTSD criteria after three sessions (d = 0.91). Psilocybin-assisted therapy produces large antidepressant effects with rapid onset — hours rather than weeks. Effect sizes exceed conventional antidepressants by more than double. FDA granted breakthrough therapy designation to MDMA in 2017.

Quickstart

# Clone and install
$ git clone https://github.com/opensynthesislabs/open-synthesis.git
$ cd open-synthesis
$ uv sync

# List available data sources
$ open-synthesis sources

# Ingest papers on a topic
$ open-synthesis ingest "psilocybin depression" --sources semantic_scholar,pubmed

# Run a synthesis (requires RunPod endpoint)
$ open-synthesis synthesize "What is the evidence for psilocybin as a treatment for MDD?"
